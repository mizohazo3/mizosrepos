Creative Command Words for AI Agents

A collection of verbs to instruct AI agents for implementation, improvement, and transformation.

Minor Adjustments & Polishing

For small improvements, clarity, and finishing touches.

  • Refine: Make small improvements to clarity, precision, or elegance.
  • Polish: Add finishing touches, smooth rough edges.
  • Tweak: Make small, subtle adjustments.
  • Hone: Sharpen or perfect something (like an argument or code snippet).
  • Fine-tune: Adjust carefully for optimal performance or effect.
  • Calibrate: Adjust for accuracy or standard.
  • Smooth: Remove abruptness or difficulties.

Improving & Strengthening

For enhancing quality, value, robustness, or effectiveness.

  • Enhance: Improve the quality, value, or extent of something.
  • Augment: Make something greater by adding to it.
  • Strengthen: Make more robust, secure, or compelling.
  • Bolster: Support or strengthen; prop up.
  • Optimize: Make as effective, perfect, or useful as possible (often for code/performance).
  • Elevate: Raise to a more important or impressive level.
  • Amplify: Increase the volume, intensity, or significance of.

Clarifying & Simplifying

For making things clearer, less complex, or more focused.

  • Clarify: Make clearer and easier to understand.
  • Simplify: Make less complex or complicated.
  • Streamline: Make more efficient or effective by simplifying procedures.
  • Distill: Extract the most important aspects or meaning.
  • Unpack: Explain or analyze in detail.
  • Elucidate: Make something clear; explain.

Adding Creativity & Style

For infusing personality, flair, or specific qualities.

  • Embellish: Make more attractive or interesting (use cautiously!).
  • Enrich: Improve or enhance the quality or supply of; make more meaningful.
  • Invigorate: Give strength or energy to; revitalize.
  • Inject [X]: Add a specific quality (e.g., Inject more personality).
  • Infuse with [Y]: Fill with a quality (e.g., Infuse with warmth).
  • Stylize: Give a particular style to.

Significant Changes & Restructuring

For noticeable revisions, reorganization, or updates.

  • Improve: (The classic) Make better.
  • Rework: Revise or reorganize.
  • Restructure: Change the fundamental structure of.
  • Reorganize: Arrange into a new structure or order.
  • Refactor: (Code specific) Restructure existing code without changing external behavior.
  • Adapt: Modify for a new use or purpose.
  • Modernize: Bring up to date with current styles, methods, or technology.

Radical Transformation & Overhaul

For fundamental, dramatic changes or complete renovations.

  • Overhaul: Take apart to examine and repair; renovate thoroughly.
  • Revamp: Give new and improved form, structure, or appearance to.
  • Reimagine: Reinterpret creatively; imagine again or differently.
  • Transform: Make a thorough or dramatic change in the form, appearance, or character.
  • Rebuild: Build again after destruction; fundamentally change.
  • Revolutionize: Change (something) radically or fundamentally.
Usage Tip: Combine these powerful verbs with specific details about *what* needs changing and *how*. For example: "Optimize the getUserData() function for faster database queries," or "Reimagine the onboarding process to be more interactive." Choose the word that best reflects the scale and nature of the change you need!
